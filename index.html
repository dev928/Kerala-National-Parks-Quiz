Kerala National Parks Quiz

1. Which is the largest national park in Kerala?
Silent Valley National Park, located in the Palakkad district, is the largest national park in Kerala, covering an area of 237.52 sq. km. It is known for its rich biodiversity and is home to the endangered lion-tailed macaque.
2. In which district is Eravikulam National Park located?
Eravikulam National Park is situated in the Idukki district of Kerala. It is famous for housing the Nilgiri Tahr, a highly endangered species of mountain goat.
3. Which national park in Kerala is known for the Nilgiri Tahr?
Eravikulam National Park is home to the largest population of Nilgiri Tahr (Nilgiritragus hylocrius), a mountain goat endemic to the Western Ghats. The park is also famous for the blooming of Neelakurinji flowers once every 12 years.
4. Which national park in Kerala is a major tiger reserve?
Periyar National Park, located in Thekkady, is a major tiger reserve in Kerala. It is also famous for its large population of Indian elephants and is centered around the Periyar Lake.
5. Which national park is part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve?
Silent Valley National Park is part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve, which spans Tamil Nadu, Kerala, and Karnataka. It is a protected area for many rare and endemic species of flora and fauna.
